City of San Mateo Municipal Code.

8.02.006 VIOLATIONS.

It is unlawful to commit any act declared by any provision of this chapter to be unlawful, to violate this chapter, or to fail to comply with the mandatory requirements of this chapter.

(Ord. No. 2005-15 § 30; Ord. No. 1990-8 § 6; Ord. No. 1987-24 § 3; Ord. 1985-27 §§ 1, 2, 1985; Ord. No. 1982-1 § 2.)
